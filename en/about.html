PBI Robot Education Center nurtures future talent through systematic robot education.

Our Vision

PBI Robot Education Center envisions 'a world where everyone can learn, build, and utilize robots.' Through hands-on education, expert instructors, and cutting-edge equipment, we provide optimal robot education for learners of all levels, from kids to corporations.

Core Educational Values

Hands-on Focus

Balanced theory and practice education, pursuing project-based learning where students build and code.

Inclusive Education

Creating an open educational environment where anyone can learn robotics regardless of age or background.

Tech Innovation

Continuously incorporating the latest robotics technology and teaching methods to provide forward-looking education.
